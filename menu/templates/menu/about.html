About RestoDelight

A legacy of taste, tradition, and togetherness since 2005.

Our Legacy

Founded in 2005, RestoDelight has become a beloved culinary destination in Thrissur. Our story began with a simple dream: to bring people together over delicious food, warm hospitality, and a cozy atmosphere. Over the years, we have grown from a small family-run eatery to a vibrant restaurant known for our commitment to quality and tradition.

  • 2005: RestoDelight opens its doors in Irinjalakuda.
  • 2010: Awarded "Best New Restaurant" in Thrissur.
  • 2018: Expanded to include a beautiful outdoor dining area.
  • 2025: Celebrated 1,00,000th guest with a grand food festival.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Alex Gourmet brings over 20 years of culinary expertise, blending classic techniques with modern flavors. His dedication to excellence is at the heart of every dish we serve. Chef Alex believes in the magic of fresh ingredients and the joy of sharing a meal with loved ones.

“Cooking is not just about recipes, it’s about creating memories.”

Chef Alex Gourmet
